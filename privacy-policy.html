Introduction & Scope

SV Infonet Solution Private Limited ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the SV eCAF mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when our authorised field sales agents ("Users") use the App.

This App is strictly for internal business use by employees and authorised agents of SV Infonet Solution Private Limited. It is not available to the general public. Access requires valid company-issued credentials.

🔒
By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use and contact your manager to surrender access.

3

How We Use the Information

  • To verify agent identity and grant authorised App access
  • To process and store Customer Application Forms on behalf of SV Infonet
  • To generate PDF documents of completed CAF forms for official records
  • To transmit completed forms to the Company's internal backend system
  • To comply with regulatory requirements for telecom/ISP service providers
  • To improve App functionality and troubleshoot technical issues
4

Data Storage & Security

All data collected through the App is stored on SV Infonet's private Supabase database hosted on secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure. Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections.

🛡️
Security measures include: Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, strict access controls limited to authorised Company personnel, regular security audits, and no public access to the backend.
5

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Internal Company departments — for processing customer service applications
  • Regulatory authorities — when required by law, court order, or government directive
  • Infrastructure providers — Supabase (our backend host), bound by strict confidentiality agreements and data processing terms

Data Retention

Customer application data is retained for as long as required to fulfil the purpose of service provisioning and to comply with legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations applicable to telecom/ISP operators in India.

Agent login data is retained for the duration of the agent's employment or engagement with the Company and deleted promptly upon termination.
